# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Target User
The exact target user is a **VP of Engineering** or **CTO** at a Seed-to-Series B startup (20-150 employees). These are individuals who manage the engineering budget but are far enough removed from individual developer IC work that they don't manually track which developer actually uses their Cursor or Copilot seat. They care about burn rate and extending runway, but AI spend is usually grouped under a generic "software" line item on their corporate card.

## Trigger Moment
They are likely looking at their monthly Ramp/Brex/Stripe statement, seeing a surprisingly high charge from OpenAI or GitHub, and thinking, "Are we actually using all of these seats?" or "Why did our Anthropic bill spike?" They might be Googling "how to track copilot usage" or "openai team vs enterprise pricing".

## Where They Hang Out
- Private Slack communities: CTO Craft, Rands Leadership Slack, YC Founders Slack.
- Specific subreddits: r/SaaS, r/ExperiencedDevs (lurking), r/startup.
- X (Twitter) Lists: Engineering Leaders, VCs discussing startup burn rate.

## First 100 Users in 30 Days ($0 Budget)
To get the first 100 users, I would use a highly targeted, manual outbound approach combined with "drop-in" utility:
1. **The "Tear-down" Thread:** I would run 5 audits of well-known public companies (or "hypothetical" Series A startups) and post the specific math on Twitter and LinkedIn. "A typical 50-person eng team wastes $18k/yr on redundant AI tools. Here's the exact breakdown. Test your own stack here."
2. **Cold DM with Value:** DM engineering leaders on LinkedIn with: "Hey [Name], noticed [Company] is scaling eng. I built a free calculator that finds wasted spend on Cursor/Copilot/OpenAI seats. Takes 30 secs, no email required to see results."
3. **The YC/Incubator Play:** Reach out to partners at accelerators (YC, Techstars). "We built a tool to help your portcos extend their runway by auditing AI spend. Here is a unique link for your cohort."

## The Unfair Distribution Channel
Credex’s unfair advantage is its existing ledger of AI credit liquidity. Credex can uniquely offer *immediate remediation*. Other tools just tell you that you're overspending; Credex can say, "You're overspending by $5k, and *we have the discounted credits right now* to fix it. Click here to claim them."
